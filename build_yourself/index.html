Built from scratch by you

Mirobot has been designed to be easy to assemble with minimal tools required. After assembling it, children will be more engaged in using it and have more of a sense of achievement

Self-assembly Chassis

Mirobot is laser-cut from a single small piece of MDF and can be assembled in a matter of minutes. There is only one screw and everything pegs together.

Solder your own circuit

The brain of Mirobot is an Arduino-based circuit board that uses through-hole components so it's a straightforward task to solder it yourself. It's easily reprogrammable using just a serial cable

Make your own from scratch

If you're feeling like going through the entire process, you can download the PCB files and get them made and laser cut the chassis to really build everything yourself.

Easily modified

It's easy to add in things like extra sensors and actuators because the chassis is made from MDF and the PCB exposes extra pins just in case. Mirobot is a first step - the next are up to you.
