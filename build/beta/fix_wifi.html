Instructions

This should enable Mirobot to get on your WiFi network. Follow these instructions:

  • Load this page - you've already done this because you're looking at it!
  • Switch your WiFi network to the "mirobot" network
  • Wait 20 seconds
  • Click the "Fix WiFi" button in the box below
  • You should see a "Saved Successfully" message
  • Click the "Restart" button
  • Wait 20 seconds
  • Visit http://10.10.100.254/ and configure Mirobot to join your Wi~Fi network
  • If it doesn't load straight away, remove the power to do a hard reset and try again
